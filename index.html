Our purpose is to provide a safe and creative environment to our aspiring students to connect with their native roots by educating them in the Tamil language, traditions, and culture. Our curriculum is rich, broad, and balanced to simulate interest in Tamil from the early years. Our MissionNunmaan will foster curiosity to encourage a deep understanding of the Tamil language, which will allow students to carry Tamil into the next generation. Our VisionNunmaan has teachers with over 12 years of experience in Tamil education. Our team will use the American Tamil Academy curriculum to not only enrich your children, but also challenge them. We have curated our own innovative speaking and reading program to ensure our students become confident with the Tamil language.
